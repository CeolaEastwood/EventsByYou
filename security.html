Data Security 101



Here at events by you we strive to keep your information safe. Did you know that your computer can send information about the browser, operating system, compatibility issues, and if you have a good internet connection? It’s also possible to get information about the monitor such as the height, width, color depth, and pixels.


However, one of the best things you can do is change your passwords. While experts disagree on the frequency, you should always reset passwords as soon as you think your account is compromised. Learn more about it here


It may seem annoying, but two-factor authentication has become more common as more security breaches happen. This is an extensive article for more information


Something most people don’t think about is public wi-fi. The more of them you connect with the higher the chance of your information will the stolen. Take advantage of the new security features on Chrome and when in doubt, try to use a data plan. More info here.


So please humor us if we ask you to change your password.
